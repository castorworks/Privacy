# Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to 6DegreesHub (App Store name: `6DegreesHub`, referred to below as the "App").

The App is a productivity tool for creating, editing, exploring, and managing knowledge graphs and relationship networks. We take privacy seriously and use a local-first approach wherever possible. Please read this policy carefully before using the App.

## 1. What Information We Process

### 1.1 Content You Create and Store Locally

When you use the App, you may create, edit, and store the following information on your device:

- Spaces, nodes, relations, and custom types
- Node names, notes, relation labels, and graph structure
- Images you manually select for nodes
- App settings such as display preferences, language, and app lock status
- Local AI usage statistics such as request counts, token usage, latency, and failure types

This information is stored locally on your device by default and is used to provide core features such as graph creation, editing, search, import/export, sharing, and settings management.

### 1.2 iCloud Sync Data

If you are signed in to iCloud on your Apple device and CloudKit sync is available, the App may sync the following data across your devices through Apple's iCloud/CloudKit private database:

- Space information
- Nodes, relations, custom types, and related text content
- Node images
- Other graph-related structured data

This data is used only to enable cross-device sync. It is not used for advertising and is not used for cross-app tracking.

### 1.3 Data Related to AI Features

The App supports user-configured third-party large language model (LLM) services. Data is sent to the third-party AI provider you choose only when you actively configure and use AI features.

The content that may be sent includes:

- The text prompt you enter
- Relevant graph context, such as space names, node names, relation labels, notes, and custom types
- Voice command transcripts
- Graph snapshots or excerpts required to generate import drafts, answer graph questions, suggest repairs, or refine voice input

This data is used only to complete AI requests you initiate. The App does not automatically send your graph content to any third-party AI provider simply because the App is installed.

Please note that third-party AI providers may process submitted data under their own privacy policies. If your graph contains sensitive personal information, use AI features with caution.

### 1.4 Microphone and Speech Recognition

When you actively use voice command features, the App may request:

- Microphone access
- Speech recognition access

These permissions are used only to capture and transcribe spoken graph commands. Speech recognition capabilities are provided by Apple system services and related system-level processing is subject to Apple's own terms and privacy policies.

The App does not use your raw audio for advertising purposes and does not upload raw recordings to our own servers. Transcript text is sent to your configured third-party AI provider only when you actually use AI-related voice features, as described in the "Data Related to AI Features" section above.

### 1.5 Face ID / Touch ID App Lock

If you enable the app lock feature, the App uses Apple's biometric authentication capabilities, such as Face ID or Touch ID, to protect access to local data.

- The App does not read, store, or upload your facial data or fingerprint data
- Biometric data is processed by Apple at the device level
- The App only receives the result of whether authentication succeeded

### 1.6 In-App Purchases and Restore Purchases

The App uses Apple StoreKit to provide in-app purchases, restore purchases, and entitlement validation.

- Payment information is handled by Apple
- The App does not store your bank card or other payment information
- The App determines your Pro entitlement locally based on purchase status returned by Apple

### 1.7 API Keys

If you configure an API key for AI services, the App stores that key in the device Keychain so it can send requests to the AI provider you selected.

- API keys are not uploaded to our own servers
- You can modify or clear the relevant configuration in the App settings

## 2. How We Use Information

We process the information described above only for the following purposes:

- To provide graph creation, editing, search, Q&A, import/export, and sharing features
- To sync graph data across your devices
- To respond to AI requests you initiate
- To protect access to the App
- To determine Pro purchase status and enable corresponding features
- To maintain required local settings and local usage statistics

## 3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information to advertisers and do not use data for cross-app tracking.

Your data may be shared with or transmitted to the following service providers in the following situations:

- Apple iCloud / CloudKit: to sync graph data across your devices
- Apple StoreKit: to process purchases, restore purchases, and entitlement validation
- Apple speech-related system services: to transcribe voice input you initiate
- Third-party AI providers you configure: to complete AI requests you initiate

Other than the cases above, we do not upload your graph content to our own servers as part of normal App use.

## 4. Data Retention

We generally retain data as follows:

- Local data: kept on your device until you delete the content, reset app data, or uninstall the App
- iCloud sync data: kept in your iCloud private storage until you delete the related content or disable the relevant sync environment
- AI usage statistics: kept locally on your device until you manually reset them in the App
- API keys: kept in the device Keychain until you modify or remove them, or until the system deletes the related Keychain item

For data sent to third-party AI providers, retention periods are determined by those providers and are outside our direct control.

## 5. Your Choices and Controls

You can control your data in the following ways:

- Do not use AI features, or do not configure an AI service
- Delete nodes, relations, spaces, or node images
- Use the App's export features to export your own data
- Manage microphone, speech recognition, and iCloud permissions in iOS Settings
- Enable or disable Face ID / Touch ID app lock in the App settings
- Modify or clear AI service configuration in the App settings

If the laws in your region give you rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of personal information, you may also contact us through the support information shown on the App Store page.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the App.

## 7. International Transfers

If you use third-party AI services, related data may be transferred to and processed in the countries or regions where those providers operate. Different providers may be subject to different privacy rules. Please review their policies before use.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time based on feature changes, legal requirements, or product updates. If material changes are made, we may publish an updated version through the in-app link, support page, or App version updates. The updated policy becomes effective on the date it is published.

## 9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how the App processes data, please contact us through the support link on the App Store page or the developer's public support page.
