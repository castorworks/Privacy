# Privacy Policy

## Data Collection

SeasonGrid does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

## What This Means

- **No analytics**: We do not track app usage or user behavior
- **No personal information**: We do not collect names, emails, or any identifiable information
- **No network requests**: The app operates entirely offline with no server communication
- **No third-party services**: We do not integrate any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs

## Local Data

All data you create within SeasonGrid (assembly code, settings, etc.) remains on your device and is never transmitted externally.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the App Store.
